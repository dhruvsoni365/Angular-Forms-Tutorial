About Fibonacci Series

The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, usually starting with 0 and 1.

Formula: F(n) = F(n-1) + F(n-2), where F(0) = 0 and F(1) = 1

As the sequence progresses, the ratio between consecutive terms approaches the Golden Ratio (φ ≈ 1.618), a mathematical constant found throughout nature and art.
